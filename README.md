# DungeonWard
This page describes the privacy policy required by Google Play for a game Dungeon Ward.

Privacy policy:

Effective date: 2022-02-01
Last updated: 2025-12-20

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Dungeon Ward (the “Game”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Game.

Developer / Data Controller (EU/EEA/UK): František Liška (personal developer account)
Address: Vochov 330, 330 23 Vochov, Czechia (CZ)
Website: https://dungeonward.com/
Contact: support@dungeonward.com

1) What this policy covers
This policy applies to the Dungeon Ward mobile application on Android and iOS and the services used by the Game (ads, mediation, analytics, crash reporting, and in-app purchases).

2) Summary
- Ads: Google AdMob + Unity LevelPlay (ironSource mediation)
- Analytics: Unity Analytics
- Crash reporting: Firebase Crashlytics
- Google Play Games Services: login/leaderboards/achievements/cloud saves (where enabled)
- In-app purchases: handled by Google Play / Apple App Store
- EU/EEA/UK: consent for personalized ads where required; iOS may show ATT prompt

3) Information we process
A) Automatically collected (device & usage)
- Device info (model, OS version, language, region settings)
- App info (version, session timestamps, basic diagnostics/performance)
- Approximate location from IP (country/region level)
- Advertising/analytics identifiers: AAID (Android), IDFA (iOS only if allowed via ATT)
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, error codes, frequency capping)

B) In-app purchases
- Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchase
- We may receive purchase validation data (product ID, timestamp, receipt/token)
- We do not receive full payment card details

C) Google Play Games Services (optional)
If enabled, Google processes data required to provide login/leaderboards/achievements/cloud saves under Google’s policies.

D) Firebase Crashlytics
Crashlytics collects crash reports (stack traces), timestamps, app state, and device/app info to help us fix crashes and improve stability.

4) How we use information
- Operate the Game
- Show ads and measure performance
- Improve gameplay and stability
- Validate purchases and restore purchases
- Prevent fraud/abuse and invalid traffic

5) Sharing
We may share information with:
- Google (AdMob, Play Games, Play Billing)
- Unity / ironSource (LevelPlay) and mediated ad networks
- Unity Analytics
- Firebase Crashlytics
- Apple (iOS purchases)
- Authorities if required by law

We do not sell personal data for money. Advertising partners may process data for interest-based advertising where you consent.

6) GDPR legal bases (EU/EEA/UK)
- Consent (personalized ads where required)
- Contract (providing the Game and purchased content)
- Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, basic measurement and improvements)
- Legal obligations (where applicable)

7) Your choices
- Google Ads Settings: https://adssettings.google.com/authenticated
- Android: reset/limit Advertising ID in device settings
- iOS: manage ATT tracking permission in iOS Settings

8) Your rights (EU/EEA/UK)
You may have rights to access, delete, correct, object, restrict, port data, and withdraw consent. Contact: support@dungeonward.com

9) Children
The Game is not intended for children under the age where parental consent is required by law. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us.

10) EU representative / DPO
The developer is established in the EU (Czechia). An EU representative is not required. We have not appointed a DPO. Contact: support@dungeonward.com
